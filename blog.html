My View on Cryptography

Kerry Spencer / July 14th, 2022

I think that I have been impacted by cryptography in a major way because without it, we would not have been able to learn about civilizations of the past. Being able to learn about these societies has allowed people to further their education of history and ancient times. I think that whether cryptography is positive or negative depends on the context in which it is used. Cryptography can be a very helpful tool, whether it is used for good or bad purposes. When cryptography is used during wartimes to warn troops of an enemy attack, that would be seen as a positive use. If the information that being hidden by cryptography is dangerous, then that would be seen as a negative use.
