Kerry Spencer's Biography

My name is Kerry Spencer and I am a sophomore at Syracuse University majoring in Information Management & Technology. I am from Westchester, NY but I live in Nashville, TN.

I decided to become a TA for this class because of how much I enjoyed my experience with IT Girls. I participated in the retreat in the Fall of 2019, and it was such an incredible experience. It is one of the main reasons as to why I chose to attend Syracuse University, and it helped me further my interest in IT.

When it comes to specific interests in technology, I am very interested in data analytics. Data is very important in many different career fields, such as sports, healthcare, and retail. I am very fascinated by the idea of using data to make the world a better place.

I love spending time with friends, going to games in the Dome, and listening to music. I love watching TV, and my favorite shows are Stranger Things and Criminal Minds.
